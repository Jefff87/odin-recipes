Lasagne

Description

A recipe from my home in Emilia-Romagna, Italy. This lasagne verde made with spinach pasta cannot be beaten. It's excellent if you want to impress some guests — or even yourself.

It may be a bit time-consuming, but it's well worth the TLC you put into it.

Ingredients

  • 5 ounces spinach - rinsed, stemmed, and dried
  • ⅝ cup semolina flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Make pasta
  2. Make ragu
  3. make béchamel
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Finish ragu
