Pork Stew

Description

This creamy pork stew recipe is one of my favorite cold-weather dishes of all time. You can add squash, Brussels sprouts, cabbage, mushrooms, or root vegetables â€” the recipe is quite versatile. If pork isn't your thing, this would be lovely with other meats, such as veal, beef, or chicken thighs. Serve over steamed rice, mashed potatoes, or noodles.

Ingredients

  • pork shoulder
  • salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • garlic
  • apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Season pork chunks generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot
  3. Place onions into the same pot
  4. Increase the heat to high and stir in mustard and horseradish
